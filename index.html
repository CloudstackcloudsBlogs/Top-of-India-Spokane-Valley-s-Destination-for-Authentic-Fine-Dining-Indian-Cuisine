Why Top of India Stands Out

Authentic Recipes

Our culinary team is dedicated to preserving the authenticity of traditional Indian cooking. The menu features a variety of dishes inspired by the diverse regions of India, from the flavorful curries of Punjab to the aromatic rice dishes of Hyderabad. The chefs rely on time-honored techniques, such as slow simmering, marination, and tandoor roasting, to bring out the rich flavors of each ingredient.

Fresh, High-Quality Ingredients

The foundation of exceptional Indian cuisine lies in the quality of its ingredients. Only the freshest produce, premium meats, and authentic spice blends are used. Vegetables are sourced locally whenever possible, while spices are imported directly from India to ensure the dishes maintain their true essence. This commitment to freshness and quality is evident in every bite.

Refined Ambiance

Our atmosphere strikes a perfect balance between elegance and comfort. The restaurant’s décor blends contemporary sophistication and traditional Indian design elements. Soft lighting, ornate wall accents, and gentle Indian music create a serene and inviting environment. Whether celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual night out, the restaurant’s setting offers a refined yet welcoming experience.

Exceptional Service

Fine dining goes beyond just the food—it’s also about the service. Our staff is known for their attentiveness and genuine hospitality. You can expect personalized service, thoughtful recommendations, and careful attention to detail from the moment you are seated. Whether you have dietary preferences, need wine pairings, or have questions about the menu, the team ensures your seamless and enjoyable experience.

Signature Dishes Bursting with Flavor

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

A classic North Indian dish, butter chicken is one of our most popular choices. This dish features tender, marinated chicken simmered in a rich tomato-based sauce infused with butter, cream, and spices. The velvety texture and harmonious balance of savory, sweet, and tangy flavors make it a must-try for newcomers and seasoned Indian food lovers.

Lamb Biryani

The lamb biryani is a standout option for those who enjoy aromatic rice dishes. Fragrant basmati rice is layered with tender, marinated lamb and slow-cooked with a medley of spices, including cardamom, cinnamon, and saffron. The dish is served with a cooling raita, which balances the warmth of the spices, creating a satisfying and well-rounded meal.

Tandoori Mixed Grill

The tandoori mixed grill offers a flavorful variety of marinated meats cooked in the traditional clay oven. The platter includes chicken, lamb, and shrimp, all infused with the smoky char from the tandoor. Each bite delivers a perfect combination of juicy tenderness and rich, earthy spices, making it an excellent choice for those seeking a diverse tasting experience.

Vegetable Korma

Vegetarians will appreciate the vegetable korma, a dish that celebrates the natural flavors of fresh vegetables. Cooked in a creamy cashew-based sauce, this dish is infused with fragrant spices, creating a rich and satisfying flavor profile. It pairs beautifully with garlic naan or saffron rice, making it a filling and flavorful option.

Garlic Naan

No Indian meal is complete without naan, and garlic naan is a customer favorite. This soft, pillowy bread is baked in the tandoor and brushed with butter and minced garlic. It is the perfect companion for curries, as it soaks up the rich sauces while adding a subtle garlic kick.

Perfect Pairings: Wine and Cocktails with Indian Cuisine

Wine Selections

Pairing wine with Indian cuisine can elevate the dining experience by enhancing the flavors of the dishes. We offer a curated wine list designed to complement the complex spices and bold flavors of its menu.

  • White Wines: Crisp white wines such as Riesling or Sauvignon Blanc pair well with spiced curries, as their acidity cuts through the richness of the sauce.
  • Red Wines: For heartier dishes like lamb rogan josh or beef vindaloo, bold reds such as Zinfandel or Syrah enhance the depth and warmth of the spices.

Signature Cocktails

Tips for the Best Dining Experience

  1. Make a Reservation: To avoid wait times, especially on weekends and holidays, booking a table in advance is recommended.
  2. Share Dishes: Indian cuisine is best enjoyed in a family style. Ordering a variety of dishes allows you to experience multiple flavors and textures.
  3. Ask About Spice Levels: Whether you prefer mild, medium, or extra spicy, our chefs can customize the spice level of most dishes to suit your preference.
  4. Try the Chef’s Specials: The restaurant frequently offers seasonal specials and unique dishes. Ask your server for recommendations to discover something new.
  5. Pair with Indian Bread: Complement your curry dishes with freshly baked naan or roti to fully enjoy the flavors of the sauce.

Vegetarian and Vegan Options for Every Palate

At Top of India, we are proud to offer a flavorful selection of vegetarian and vegan dishes prepared with the same dedication to taste and quality as our meat-based options. Whether you follow a plant-based diet or simply enjoy exploring meatless meals, our menu features a variety of satisfying choices inspired by authentic Indian cuisine.

  • Chana Masala: Tender chickpeas simmered in a tangy tomato and onion sauce, enhanced with aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala. This hearty dish pairs perfectly with basmati rice or warm naan.
  • Baingan Bharta: Roasted eggplant, peeled and mashed, then slow-cooked with onions, tomatoes, and a blend of spices. The result is a rich, smoky dish that pairs wonderfully with roti or paratha.
  • Aloo Gobi: A comforting combination of potatoes and cauliflower sautéed with turmeric, cumin, and ginger. This mildly spiced curry offers a warm, satisfying flavor.
  • Dal Tadka: Yellow lentils cooked until tender, then tempered with garlic, cumin, and mustard seeds. The finishing touch of sizzling spices adds depth to this hearty and nourishing dish.

Whether you’re in the mood for a savory curry or a satisfying lentil dish, our vegetarian and vegan options promise to deliver bold flavors and authentic Indian taste in every bite.
